Latin UDHR Test Sample
======================

Reference Text (Classical orthography with macrons):
----------------------------------------------------
Omnēs hominēs dignitate et iūre līberī et parēs nāscuntur, ratiōne et
cōnscientiā praeditī sunt, quāpropter frāternē inter sē gerant oportet.

Reference Text (Common orthography without macrons):
----------------------------------------------------
Omnes homines dignitate et iure liberi et pares nascuntur, ratione et
conscientia praediti sunt, quapropter fraterne inter se gerant oportet.

English Translation:
--------------------
All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights. They are
endowed with reason and conscience and should act towards one another in
a spirit of brotherhood.
